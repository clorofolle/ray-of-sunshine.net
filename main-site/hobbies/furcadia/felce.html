Felce/Tilther

OOC

My current main alt is called Felce!

From 2008 to 2015 I used to go everywhere on the internet as Tilther, as it was the original name of this specific character/sona. It was a name created on a whim by a kid, it has no specific meaning. I'm still attached to it but I don't use it as a handle anymore. However, I still call this character that way.

If I'm online on Furc, it's probably on Felce. I spend my time dreamhopping, exploring, and winding down. But I usually love chatting a bit and making friends! I especially love go exploring dreams with other people, or being shown around someone's dream. Invite me in, please!!

When I am idle, I'm usually in Challanges, Naia Green, Sanctuary, or Theriopolis. If you can find me, say hello!

I'm a Socializer and uninterested in serious roleplay or long term projects, but this furre is a character before being a sona! I enjoy light hearted Persona Play (or used to - it's been ages!) and find it fun to explore dreams while impersonating a silly furre character. Plus, I've been accumulating lore on this silly little character since I was a kid!

IC

The character is being heavily reworked - as I grow, so do they. This will be updated when I have a more coherent image of who Tilther is now!
